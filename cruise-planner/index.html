What should a cruise planner app include?

A strong cruise planner covers daily schedules, port days, bookings, packing, and shared planning for travel companions—without forcing you to juggle multiple apps.

Can I share my cruise plan with family?

Yes. SeaDays Plan supports shared workspaces so everyone sees the same itinerary, packing list, and to-dos in real time.

Does SeaDays work offline on the ship?

SeaDays caches key trip data for weak onboard Wi‑Fi, so your itinerary and notes stay available when connectivity drops at sea.
