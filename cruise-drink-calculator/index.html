How does a cruise drink package calculator work?

You enter sailing length, typical drinks per day, and package price. The calculator estimates break-even servings and whether the package beats buying drinks individually.

Do all cruise lines price packages the same?

No. Lines and regions price packages differently. SeaDays models common package tiers so you can sanity-check the upsell at booking or embarkation.
