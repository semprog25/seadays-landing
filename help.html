Frequently Asked Questions

What is SeaDays?

SeaDays is a comprehensive cruise planning app that helps you track your voyages, earn XP and achievements, connect with fellow cruisers, and manage every aspect of your maritime adventures.

How do I add a cruise?

You can add cruises through the "Plan" tab in the app. Enter your booking details or connect directly with supported cruise lines to import your itinerary automatically.

What are XP and Achievements?

XP (Experience Points) are earned by completing cruises, visiting ports, and engaging with the community. Achievements are special badges unlocked by reaching milestones in your cruising journey.

Is SeaDays free?

Yes! SeaDays offers a free tier with core features. Premium tiers unlock unlimited cruises, advanced statistics, and exclusive benefits.

How do I contact support?

Email us at support@seadays.semprog.de for any questions, bug reports, or feature requests. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.

Can I export my data?

Yes! You can export all your cruise data, statistics, and photos from your account settings at any time.
