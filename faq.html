Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SeaDays

Q What is SeaDays?
SeaDays is a comprehensive cruise planning app that helps you organize your cruise vacations, track your maritime adventures, earn XP and achievements, connect with fellow cruisers, and share your experiences with a vibrant community.
Q How does the XP and leveling system work?
Earn XP by adding cruises, visiting ports, completing challenges, and engaging with the community. Progress through 30 Nautical Ranks from Landlubber to Fleet Admiral. Premium subscriptions offer XP multipliers to level up faster!
Q Is SeaDays free to use?
Yes! SeaDays offers a free tier that includes basic cruise tracking, XP earning, and community access. Premium subscriptions (Sailor, Captain, Admiral) unlock additional features like unlimited cruises, XP multipliers, exclusive badges, and advanced maps.
Q What are the different subscription tiers?
Free: Track up to 5 cruises, basic XP & leveling
Sailor ($4.99/mo): 15 cruises, 2x XP, premium badges
Captain ($9.99/mo): 50 cruises, 3x XP, all premium features
Admiral ($19.99/mo): Unlimited cruises, 5x XP, VIP support
Q Can I use SeaDays with my crew/travel group?
Absolutely! SeaDays includes crew collaboration features. Create a crew, invite members, share itineraries, chat in real-time, and plan your cruise adventure together. Perfect for families, friends, or solo cruisers looking to meet new people!
Q How do I track my cruise history?
Simply add your past and upcoming cruises to your profile. SeaDays automatically tracks ports visited, days at sea, miles sailed, and creates beautiful visualizations of your maritime journey on interactive maps.
Q What kind of achievements can I unlock?
Unlock badges for visiting specific ports, completing certain numbers of cruises, reaching XP milestones, social engagement, and special events. Each badge adds to your maritime credentials and shows off your cruising expertise!
Q Is my data secure?
Yes! We use industry-standard encryption, secure authentication, and follow strict data protection practices. We never sell your personal data. Check our Privacy Policy for complete details.
Q Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. You'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your billing period.
Q Will SeaDays be available on mobile?
SeaDays is currently available as a web app and is fully optimized for mobile browsers. Native iOS and Android apps are coming soon! Join our waitlist to be notified when they launch.
Q How do I contact support?
You can reach our support team at support@seadays.app. Premium subscribers get priority support with faster response times.
Q What is the Points Shop?
Earn points by using SeaDays and spend them in our Points Shop on exclusive rewards, premium features, custom themes, and special items. It's our way of rewarding active cruisers!
