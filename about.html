About SeaDays

Revolutionizing cruise planning, one voyage at a time

Our Story

SeaDays was born from a simple observation: cruise planning shouldn't be complicated. We noticed that cruisers were juggling multiple apps, spreadsheets, and paper documents just to organize their maritime adventures. There had to be a better way.

Our team of passionate cruisers and developers came together to create the ultimate cruise planning companion. We combined comprehensive tracking features with gamification elements, social connectivity, and beautiful design to make cruise planning not just easier, but genuinely fun.

Today, SeaDays helps cruisers plan their perfect voyages, track their maritime journeys, connect with fellow seafarers, and earn rewards for their adventures. We're just getting started on our mission to become the world's most beloved cruise planning platform.

Our Mission

To empower cruisers worldwide with the tools, community, and inspiration they need to plan unforgettable maritime adventures. We believe that every cruise should be a seamless journey from planning to boarding, and SeaDays is here to make that happen.
