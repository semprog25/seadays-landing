Frequently asked questions

What is a cruise roll call?

A roll call is a group for one ship on one sailing date. Cruisers introduce themselves, ask questions, and plan meetups before and during the voyage.

How do I find my roll call in SeaDays?

Open Crew, search your ship and sailing date, and join the roll call for your voyage. You can post introductions and chat with other guests on the same sailing.

Is SeaDays only for roll calls?

No. SeaDays is an all-in-one cruise planner app with roll calls, itineraries, budget tracking, ship and port discovery, and community features.
