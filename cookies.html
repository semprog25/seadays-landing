Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 30, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you use SeaDays. They help us provide a better experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our app.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Required for the app to function properly (login, security)

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with SeaDays

Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and customizations

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may limit functionality.
