Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 9, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you use SeaDays. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our app.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for app functionality, authentication, and security Session / 1 year
Performance Cookies Help us understand how users interact with SeaDays 1 year
Preference Cookies Remember your settings and customizations 1 year
Analytics Cookies Collect data about app usage to improve our services 2 years

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in to your account
  • Remember your preferences (theme, language, settings)
  • Understand how you use SeaDays to improve features
  • Provide personalized cruise recommendations
  • Analyze app performance and fix issues
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

Managing Cookies

You have control over cookies through your device settings:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies
  • Mobile Settings: iOS and Android have privacy settings for app tracking
  • App Preferences: Adjust analytics preferences in SeaDays settings

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect app functionality.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Authentication providers (Supabase)
  • Analytics services
  • Payment processors
  • Map and location services

These third parties have their own cookie policies that we recommend reviewing.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We encourage you to review this page regularly.
