Connect With Fellow Cruisers

Meet travelers on your sailing, share cruise tips, and discover experiences from the SeaDays community.

SeaDays brings cruisers together before and during your voyage. Use these tools in the app to plan meetups, share honest reviews, and get the most out of every port and ship.

Roll Calls

Find and join roll calls for your exact sailing. Introduce yourself, coordinate private excursions, and meet other guests before you board. Roll calls are organized by ship and date so you can connect with the right travelers.

Ship Reviews

Read and write detailed reviews of cruise ships. Rate cabins, dining, entertainment, and overall atmosphere based on your experience. Help others choose the right ship and know what to expect on board.

Port Reviews

Discover what to do at each port from real travelers. Share your favorite beaches, excursions, and local tips. Browse port guides and reviews to plan your days ashore and make the most of every stop.

Crew Chat

Chat with fellow cruisers on your sailing in real time. Plan meetups, share daily updates, and swap recommendations for shows, dining, and activities. Stay connected throughout your voyage.
