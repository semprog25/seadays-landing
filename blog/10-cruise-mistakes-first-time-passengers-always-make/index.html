10 Cruise Mistakes First-Time Passengers Always Make

Seadays Mar 14, 2026 1 min read

Trying to Do Everything

Cruise ships offer hundreds of activities, restaurants, shows, and events. Many first-time cruisers try to experience everything.

Instead of rushing from activity to activity, experienced cruisers recommend slowing down and enjoying sea days.
