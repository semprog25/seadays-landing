What Happens If You Miss Your Cruise Ship in Port?

Seadays Mar 16, 2026 1 min read

The Ship Will Not Wait

Cruise ships operate on strict schedules.

If passengers do not return before the departure time, the ship usually leaves the port without them. Cruise ships must follow port schedules and cannot delay departures for individual passengers.

How Passengers Catch Up

Passengers who miss the ship usually need to travel to the next port of call.

This may involve:

  • booking last-minute flights
  • arranging transportation to the next port
  • contacting the cruise line for assistance

Avoiding the Situation

Cruise travelers often return to the ship well before departure time.

Planning excursions and port activities carefully helps avoid missing the ship.
