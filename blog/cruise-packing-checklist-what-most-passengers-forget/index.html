Most Forgotten Cruise Items

Frequent cruisers often forget these items:

  • Motion sickness tablets
  • Magnetic hooks for cabin walls
  • Power strip (non-surge)
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Waterproof phone case
  • Day bag for port visits
