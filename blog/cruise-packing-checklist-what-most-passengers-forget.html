Most Forgotten Cruise Items

Frequent cruisers often forget these items:

  • Motion sickness tablets
  • Magnetic hooks for cabin walls
  • Power strip (non-surge)
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Waterproof phone case
  • Day bag for port visits

Quick Packing Table

ItemWhy It Helps
Magnetic hooksCabin walls are metal
Waterproof phone pouchUseful during excursions
Power stripCruise cabins have few outlets
Reusable bottleStay hydrated on deck
