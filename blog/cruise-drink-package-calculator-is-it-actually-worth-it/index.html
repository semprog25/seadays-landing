Why Cruise Drink Packages Confuse Many Travelers

Cruise drink packages promise unlimited cocktails, beer, wine, soda, and specialty coffee. At first glance they seem like a great deal.

However, many cruise travelers are surprised when they see the price. Most drink packages cost between $60 and $120 per person per day, which can quickly add up during a cruise vacation.

Understanding how much you actually drink during sea days and port days can help determine whether the package makes financial sense.

Average Cruise Drink Prices

Typical cruise ship drink prices include:

  • Cocktails: $10–$14
  • Beer: $7–$9
  • Wine: $9–$15
  • Specialty coffee: $4–$6
  • Soda: $3–$4

Sea days usually involve more onboard spending, while port days may reduce the number of drinks you purchase onboard.

Using a Drink Package Calculator

Many cruise travelers now use tools like the SeaDays cruise planner and drink package calculator to estimate their drink spending before the trip.

By planning ahead, cruisers can compare package prices with their expected daily drink habits and make a more informed decision.
